About Me

I’m Victor Waddell, a dedicated software developer with a passion for technology and collaboration. My journey began as a Junior IT and Development Consultant at Dietrich Computing and Operations from fall 2021 to fall 2023. Starting as first-line support, I quickly developed my skills and transitioned into a junior AI development and consulting role, where I contributed to the adoption of generative AI, laying the groundwork for CMU's first AI-based classroom pilots in 2023.

Throughout my time there, I’ve been recognized for my ability to remain calm and think quickly in high-pressure situations, especially when assisting demanding users. My communication skills have allowed me to convey complex technical information in a way that’s accessible to everyone, a trait that has been invaluable in both professional and personal settings.

Outside of work, I’m deeply passionate about video games, which not only fuel my love for technology but also connect me with communities that share my interests. I believe in the power of relationships, whether it’s through collaborative work environments or the connections I build in my personal life. I approach every challenge with a determination to give my all, continuously striving to learn and grow.
